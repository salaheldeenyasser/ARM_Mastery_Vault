## Interview Strategy

> **Technical interviews are conversations, not exams.**
>
> When given a problem:
> 1. Clarify requirements (interrupt-driven or polling? single-core or RTOS?)
> 2. Think out loud — show your reasoning
> 3. Start with the simplest solution, then optimize
> 4. Identify tradeoffs (power vs speed, simplicity vs safety)
> 5. Mention real pitfalls (volatile, alignment, priority inversion)
